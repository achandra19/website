In the nuances of life
A man might deviate himself
Off the continuous path
Onto one seemingly more dangerous
But there comes no gain without risk



Tread fast
The trail masks no emotion
But simply amplifies
The growing discomfort
Of the path less taken



Regret stays king
Whilst fear is the prince
But you
Simply a pawn
Of both beings lay be



The king strikes your dreams
Shatter and break as they might
The path grows colder
With every milestone
Left no reason to continue



The heat of the August sun strikes
Those who have traveled together
Those who have avoided the beaten path
Bathe in the sweet light
Free from the king and the prince

Only I
The man traveling on the beaten path
Am left frozen to the touch
Regret all but deliverance
Deteriorating
