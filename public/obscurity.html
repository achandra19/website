I lie awake at night
Wondering which string
Amongst the armada
Weaves through the emptiness
And find its home in a well of wisdom



Though lie you might
And think you do
Have you accomplished
What Force
Has divined you?



But how does one
Find the answers
When the fog within
Thickens and grows
Obscuring your vision



Do you know which string
Is weaving where?
And what lies
Beyond this darkness?
Perhaps you have lost something sacred



Have you looked through nature?
The medicine of early man
Granting you clarity
Beyond measure
Finally the fog lifted!



Have you found what you seek?
Clearly the strings are visible
Yet the questions of the mind
Outnumber our perception
And lead us astray



Ignorance is bliss they say
Well I suppose living in obscurity
Means you have less to worry about
And through life you'll find
The endpoint to this string's journey



However you ponder how long
This journey might be
And not all journeys are made alike
Perhaps the string guiding your life
Meets despair



So you must know the answers
A control-freak!
And so the flesh of the earth
Becomes a routine
Alas clarity once more



“Where does each string end?”
You think
As you begin to learn
Deeper about yourself
Endpoint after endpoint



What are limitations?
Do you think a running machine
Powered by the soul of oneself
Stops in any sense?
Unless through Nature's might



So fly high
Seek the answers
But don't forget about the goal
Which is the main question
“Who are you?”



Yet you can't listen
Like the innocence of youth
You find meaning after meaning
Rationalizations of the past
Until your vision no longer functions



Not in the sense that you go blind
Nor that the fog blocks your sight
But rather loss of function
In this program your soul defines
To finish the journey of the string



So what have you truly gained?
Was this suitable medicine?
Perhaps I'll try it again
But just know
The clarity you seek
Is not clarity at all
In fact
It's obscurity
