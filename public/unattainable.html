Reach as you might
Far into the void
Hoping for solace
Receiving an alternative instead
In which you cannot confide in



Stay hidden
Stay close
Until your body is but a shell
Hiding your deepest secrets
In the plainest of sights



Dream as you will
Pay mind to your intuition
Caring for no one else
But the thing you need
Ever so splendid, ever so sweet



Hath no one told you
Your dream is unattainable
Fret not for there is a speckle of hope
Hidden in the plainest of sights
Reached by all but you



How unfair life is
To grant you a chance
And simply whisk it away
As if you were a child
Losing your innocence of youth



But be a child no longer
Rejoice on your growth
For many a time has passed
And months grown old
For only you to remain



The dream is attainable
Goal within grasp
One simple tug
And the prize shall be won
Settling your desires in your heart of hearts



Gone are the friends
Gone are the mentors
Gone are the parents
Gone are the foes
Gone is the speckle of hope



As the darkness consumes you
And the spirit of youth laughs
One may ask
If it were worth it to grow old so fast
Leaving the dream still intact



Try as you might
In life there are no rewinds
No chance to make amends
For you are left in the desert
Alone



Water is dry
Civilisation is bunk
There is but a flash
Of an oasis in the midst
Shimmering ever so splendid



Run as fast as your legs could travel
Waste no time
For there is no time left
In the mind of the wanderer
Grown old yet so young



Grasp the oasis
One simple tug
And the prize shall be won
Settling your desires in your heart of hearts
But you open your eyes
And are greeted by a twist of fate
Disappearance of existence
The dream fading away
Back into the void
Unattainable
