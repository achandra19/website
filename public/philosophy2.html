All throughout life
Personal ambitions are overcome
By darkened news
And gloomy skies



You wish
Life was as simple
As hunting and gathering
So you wonder why life complicates itself



From the hunters and the gatherers
Come the HR professionals
Finance analysts
Backend developers



Why do we decide to create more steps
In this seemingly simple chain we call life?
Is it to further the quality of life?
Is it to increase our understanding of life?



It is called modernisation
It means whisking away your innocence of youth
Full of bright sparkled jewels
Rich in ideals

Replacing it with a bag
Visibly and legitimately empty
Craving something to hold
But never satisfied with the contents

So you stroll throughout life
Reminiscing about the time
Your pouch wasn't pitiful
But rather marvelous



But what are you to do about it?
What choice do you even have?
Is there a button somewhere
In which you can catch a glimpse



Of sweet, sweet innocence?
I suppose not
But if there were
Would you even remember it the same
