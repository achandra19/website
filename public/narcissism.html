I look into a lake
Reflecting a vision of something so great
Yet who am I looking upon
Except for my own self and my fate



I'm capable of everything
Since my self-love soars
So I don't need anyone else
I'll write my own lore



As my love burns brighter
I start to realize
What purpose does it all serve
If entropy indeed is not the prize



Scales of judgment work
Like the clockworks eternal
Creating a balance between people
Doesn't that seem deceptional?



After what seems like years
I learn it is truly a spectrum
Narcissism possessed by all
Especially in this age in which we march to society's drum



How can you allow just anyone
To share in this warmth of love
You'd be left frozen by your own choices
But I suppose if I had met someone near a dove



Would she really accept me for who I am?
But that would be preposterous
Does this imply I don't love myself?
Perhaps the fire inside did not make me prosperous



So I contemplate and come to a conclusion



Through loss comes gain
Emotions intertwined like holy root
Entropic triumph over zero
Scales of narcissism on mute
