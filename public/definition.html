Words seem to flow
Hitting the ears of each person in the crosshairs
Which ensues the descrambling of the code



Sounds vibrate through
Gathering the attention of the select few
Who know what each word means



The big hand strikes a mark
The arrays of desks accommodated
Ready to tackle the noiseless commotion



So is the job of the undefined word
To find a home
In the ears of the heard
And put a name to each word
Save its own



Only when one finds meaning
In a seemingly noiseless word
Can there be sound
Without any sound
Just the murmurs of acceptance



Each and every being hit by the sound
Disregards the disquietude
Save one
Who is seen with tears flowing down
Like a river, for she has grasped definition
