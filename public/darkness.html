There is always a man
Who claims darkness breeds hate
But that very same principle
Makes a fertile argument of another case
Where darkness employs honesty



Deep in the dark
Where loneliness seeks refuge
Lies a being
Conjoined of love and hope
Unwilling to disclose either



The hate grows strong
The fear sinks deeper
The howls make the hairs go stiff
But there is only one being
Deep in the dark



Showing affection and desire
Rejectance of both
Breeds only further loneliness
Until the dawn of day is neither seen
Nor envisioned



Sinking deeper and deeper
Into the quicksand
There is a loss of four dimensions
For one reigns supreme
Deep in the dark

Attack positivity
But don't fret
Negativity be not your nature
But a force in between
Consists of keen predilection

Neither suffer nor rejoice
The forces showcase balance
Balance overtakes all
And leaves none other to contend
Deep in the dark
